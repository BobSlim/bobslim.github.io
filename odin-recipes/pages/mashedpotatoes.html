Mashed Potatoes

A wise man once said "mashed potatoes is dairy put together by potatoes". And this has held true throughout the ages: mashed potatoes must be 50% or more dairy, else it will not taste good.

Do not trust those who will mislead you into thinking that this dish can be made healthy. It cannot, and it is good.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Seasonings:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Italian Herbs
    • Garlic

Method

  1. Boil potatoes
  2. Add dairy and seasonings. Mash
